Why AI Safety?

My background in Fintech operations (Bankly) taught me that a "99% accurate" system is a failure if the 1% error destroys trust or causes financial ruin. I am now applying this rigorous, zero-failure mindset to AI.

I am specifically interested in AI Safety in Law and Intellectual Property. As we deploy agents to interpret legal frameworks, ensuring they do not "hallucinate" laws or exploit loopholes is a critical alignment problem. I aim to research how we can use Steerability and Constitutional AI principles to make legal agents safe for under-represented jurisdictions.

Current Research: AI Safety in Legal Governance

Adora (IPP Co-pilot)

I am currently building a prototype for Intellectual Property Protection (IPP) in emerging markets. This project serves as a testbed for AI Safety principles in the legal domain.

Legal AI has a "zero-tolerance" requirement for hallucination. My research explores how to use Structured Output (JSON schemas) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to constrain Large Language Models (LLMs) to strictly adhere to regional IP laws (e.g., Nigeria, Kenya) without fabricating statutes.
